Background: Video

It's very easy to change the background video. 

 

1. Just go to the videofolder:

2. You will find the videos in 3 formats. .mp4 .ogg and .webm . And a JPG image. You can convert your videos in this 3 format very easily via http://video.online-convert.com/ . Convert your video into this 3 format and replace the old videos and JPG screenshots. 

Currently all devices under 640px width will show the background image instead of video

You will find script code on js/script.js

Image background for mobile

For better performance in mobile. In latest version an image will show in parallax background instead of video on mobile. You can change the background image from css/style.css
